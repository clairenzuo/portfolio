PLAIN JANE - ASAP FERG FT. NICKI MINAJ
Still Striving
2017
I photoshopped two separate images of Nicki Minaj and ASAP Ferg together on a Yellow background, and added rip and crinkle textures. I also altered the typography.

ASTROWORLD ALBUM - TRAVIS SCOTT
2018
(after finding out what recently happened I will change these posters for my final portfolio) I used parts of the illustrations for the tour and removed the astroworld background from a t-shirt logo for a more retro look.

90210 - TRAVIS SCOTT
Rodeo (Expanded)
2015
One of my favorite Travis Scott songs. Initially it was all black, but I went back and inverted part of the image. I added snippets of lyrics around the page.

BORED - BILLIE EILISH
13 Reasons Why
2017
I loved that photo of Billie so I created the poster based off the image, focusing on green. Her goggles looked futuristic so I added a bubble wrap texturee instead of the wrinkle texture I usually use.

HIGHEST IN THE ROOM - TRAVIS SCOTT
JACKBOYS
2019
I really liked this song at the time. Especially the outro instrumental felt like I was floating, so I focused on skies, clouds, and emphasized the color blue.
